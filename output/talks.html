Talks:

  • Introduction to Text Mining and Topic Modelling

    In this talk, I give a brief introduction to Text Mining and Topic Modelling. I cover the basics of Information Retrieval, Information Extraction and Natural Language Processing (NLP) Techniques, as well as a detailed explanation of the Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) model.

    This talk was given in the Urban Big Data Centre (University of Glasgow).
